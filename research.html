My prime research area is computer architecture, meanwhile, I also pay close attention to the newest advances in computer vision, machine learning and artificial intelligence. I am devoted to explore the computing systems for the future. Recently, I am focusing on the following topics:
Deep learning processors: designing efficient processing engine for deep learning.
IoT Computing: exploring computing platforms for IoT.
In the past, I have worked on various topics:
User-centric computing: using user satisfaction as one of the metrics to evaluate and optimize computing systems.
Mobile computing: efficient cache design for mobile devices.
